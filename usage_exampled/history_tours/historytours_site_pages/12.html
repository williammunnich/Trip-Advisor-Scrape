About
Let a Segway be your time machine as you travel back to the days of railroads, flappers, and Prohibition-era gangsters. Our St. Paul Segway Tour explores the amazing homes of Summit Avenue and the birthplace of F. Scott Fitzgerald. Catch up on the history of the Minnesota State Capitol from its founding through its many iterations and renovations to the building we know today. Our expert guides know all the stories of historic St. Paul, including a few you won’t find in the history books. It’s almost more than we can pack into a three-hour tour.
